## Word of the Day: **Volunteer**
/ˌvɒl.ənˈtɪər/ • noun / verb  

**As a noun:** A person who freely offers to take part in an activity or perform a service.  
**As a verb:** To freely offer to do something without being forced or paid.

---

### Meaning
- **Noun:** Someone who willingly offers their time, service, or help.  
- **Verb:** To offer to do something willingly and without payment.

**Part of Speech:** noun / verb

---

### Sample Situations Used in Daily Life

1. **Community Service:**  
   "She volunteers at the local animal shelter every weekend."

2. **School Activity:**  
   "Many students volunteer to help organize the annual cultural festival."

3. **Medical Assistance:**  
   "Doctors volunteered their time to provide free health checkups."

4. **Environmental Work:**  
   "He volunteered to plant trees in the city park."

5. **Office Help:**  
   "I volunteered to help with the team’s presentation."

6. **Social Work:**  
   "Volunteers distributed food and clothes to the flood-affected families."

7. **Event Management:**  
   "She volunteered to guide participants during the marathon."

8. **Online Contribution:**  
   "He volunteers to answer questions on a programming forum."

9. **Family Activity:**  
   "Kids volunteered to help set the table for the party."

10. **Emergency Aid:**  
    "Local volunteers helped evacuate people during the storm."

---

### Memory Tip
Think of **“volunteer”** as **“voluntarily + enter”** — entering to help without being forced.

---

### Synonyms
helper, participant, contributor, assistant, altruist, willing worker

### Antonyms
paid worker, forced labor, employee (in some contexts), unwilling person

---

### Word Forms
- **Volunteered** (past tense): "She volunteered at the shelter last week."  
- **Volunteering** (present participle): "He is volunteering to help at the event."  
- **Volunteers** (plural / noun): "Many volunteers came to help."

---

### Common Phrases
- Volunteer work  
- Volunteer service  
- Community volunteer  
- To volunteer for a cause  
- Volunteer program
