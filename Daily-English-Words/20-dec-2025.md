## Word of the Day: **Manus**
/ˈmeɪ.nəs/ • noun  

A Latin word meaning “hand,” often used in English in technical, legal, and academic contexts.

---

### Meaning
**Manus** refers to “hand” in Latin and is commonly used to describe control, authority, or structure in legal, medical, and scholarly terminology.

**Part of Speech:** noun

### Memory Tip
Think of **manus** as **“manual”** or **“manipulate”** — both relate to the **hand**.
